Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

At Orbit Apartments (Orbit Solutions), we value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data in accordance with Rwanda Law Nº 058/2021 relating to the protection of personal data and privacy.

1. Data We Collect

When you book one of our rooms, we collect:

  • Full Name
  • Phone Number (for booking confirmation and WhatsApp)
  • Payment Proof (Bank slips or MoMo confirmation)

2. Purpose of Collection

We use your information solely to manage your stay, process payments, and provide customer support.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in our cloud database (Supabase) and is never shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Your Rights

Under Rwandan law, you have the right to access, rectify, or request the deletion of your personal data at any time. Contact us at info@orbitsolutions.rw for any data requests.
